InfoTech Job Market Driven Scholarship for the City University of Hong Kong 2022


On 24 June 2022, InfoTech selected and awarded InfoTech Job Market Driven Scholarships to two first-degree graduates from the Department of Computer Science and the Department of Electrical Engineering respectively from the City University of Hong Kong. The selection was based on the quality and relevancy of the final year projects among applicants in adopting IT hot skills highly sought by the contemporary IT job market. The selection panel consisted of experienced InfoTech recruitment consultants and professors/lecturers of the relevant subjects of the University.

InfoTech Job Market Driven Scholarship for the Hong Kong Polytechnic University 2022


On 18 June 2022, InfoTech selected and awarded InfoTech Job Market Driven Scholarships to two first-degree graduates from the Department of Computing of the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the selection was conducted by video interview. The selection was based on the quality and relevancy of the final year projects among applicants in adopting IT hot skills highly sought by the contemporary ICT job market. The selection panel consisted of experienced InfoTech recruitment consultants and professors/lecturers of the relevant subjects of the University.

InfoTech Shanghai staff return to work in office
The Shanghai authorities have recently lifted the citywide COVID-19 lockdown in an orderly manner, InfoTech Shanghai staff are pleased to return to work at the China Merchant Tower, Lujiazui, resume operations and services to the full extent with effect from 13 June 2002. We apologize for the inconvenience caused to client employers and jobseekers during the earlier work-from-home arrangement.

The Best Innovation and Technology Staff Recruiter / Supplier


In May 2022, InfoTech was awarded as The Best Innovation and Technology Staff Recruiter / Supplier in Hong Kong's Most Outstanding Business (HKMOB) Awards 2022, by CorpHub, one of the leading online media firms. The HKMOB Award was judged on the corporate achievements in enterprise management and governance, financial performance and market competitiveness, innovation of products and amenities as well as corporate responsibilities.
